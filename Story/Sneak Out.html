Sneak Out

After seeing the chaos that was created, you decide that the best option is to sneak out of the prison, using the chaos as a sheild. So that's what you do. You tell everyone that came for backup that you are going to sneak out of the prison, and no one thinks that's a bad idea, as this way you are less likely to get caught. You start looking around, and you see 3 ways that you can get out. All 3 of them are risky, but you think that you can make it. The first way that you can escape is by climbing over the fence. The second way that you can escape is by using the front door, which had a fight going on right infront of it, though the doors were open. The third way that you can escape is by going through the hole in the wall, which other prisoners made so everyone can escape. There are many prisonners using that escape route. The final way that you can escape is by making your own exit, but finding a military truck and just driving it through the wall. You start thinking. Should you:

Escape through the hole in the fence

Escape by the front door, using a fight as cover

Escape by using the path that other prisoners made

Make your own exit.
